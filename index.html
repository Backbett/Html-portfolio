My top 3 movies of all-time.

Uri The surgical strike

This is my favourite Movie. Uri: The Surgical Strike (2019) is a thrilling, emotional, and patriotic film that tells the true story of India's 2016 surgical strikes against terrorists in Pakistan-occupied Kashmir. The film showcases the resilience and bravery of the Indian Army, with Vicky Kaushal delivering a standout performance as Major Vihaan Singh Shergill. It blends gripping action, realistic military tactics, and emotional depth, offering a powerful tribute to the sacrifices made by soldiers. With memorable dialogues, breathtaking visuals, and a strong message of national pride, Uri is an inspiring and unifying experience that will leave you proud and moved. .

Kantara

Kantara is a film that combines folklore, intense action, emotional depth, and cultural pride into an unforgettable cinematic experience. It’s a celebration of tradition, spirituality, and the human spirit, with standout performances and a gripping story that keeps you hooked from start to finish. If you enjoy films that offer more than just entertainment—films that are rich in culture, emotion, and mystery—Kantara is a must-watch.

KGF

KGF is an unforgettable cinematic experience that combines style, action, and heart. With an iconic hero, epic storytelling, and a world filled with larger-than-life characters, the film captures the essence of power, ambition, and sacrifice. Yash's performance as Rocky has become legendary, and the film’s intense action and gripping plot make it a must-watch for anyone who enjoys high-stakes drama and blockbuster entertainment. If you’re looking for a film that delivers thrills, emotional depth, and visual grandeur, KGF is a cinematic experience you can’t miss.
